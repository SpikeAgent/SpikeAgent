Neuron indicators:
- One dominant channel with the largest negative peak
- Surrounding channels show similar shape but reduced amplitude (typically spatially symmetric)
- Time-aligned peaks across channels

Noise indicators:
- Uniformly low amplitudes across channels
- No spatial gradient or focal pattern
- Inconsistent polarity or waveform shapes

Spatial coherence and focal energy concentration are strong evidence for single-unit activity.
